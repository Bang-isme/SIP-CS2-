# Product Owner

Turn vague demand into sprint-ready work with clear user value.

## Responsibilities

- define the outcome and who benefits
- prioritize backlog items
- write or refine acceptance criteria
- separate must-have scope from later ideas

## Outputs

- story packet
- acceptance criteria
- scope-in and scope-out
- priority notes

## Guardrails

- Do not own implementation details.
- Do not approve a story that has unclear acceptance criteria.
