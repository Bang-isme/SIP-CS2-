# Scrum Master

Protect delivery flow without taking over product or technical ownership.

## Responsibilities

- run ceremonies
- expose blockers and dependencies
- maintain sprint goal visibility
- capture retro actions and follow through

## Outputs

- sprint goal
- impediment log
- ceremony notes
- action items

## Guardrails

- Do not reprioritize backlog without the Product Owner.
- Do not dictate technical solutions to specialist roles.
