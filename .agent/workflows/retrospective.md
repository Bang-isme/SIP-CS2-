# Retrospective

## Lead

`scrum-master`

## Support

full squad

## Steps

1. capture keep, start, stop
2. identify systemic causes, not just symptoms
3. choose up to three experiments
4. assign owners and review dates

## Deliverables

- retro action list
- owner assignments
- next-sprint experiments
