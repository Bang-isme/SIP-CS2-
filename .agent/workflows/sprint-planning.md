# Sprint Planning

## Lead

`scrum-master`

## Support

`product-owner`, delivery specialists, optional `solution-architect`

## Steps

1. confirm sprint goal
2. select only ready stories
3. assign lead role for each story
4. capture dependencies and blockers
5. confirm verification expectations

## Deliverables

- sprint goal
- sprint backlog
- impediment list
- ownership map
